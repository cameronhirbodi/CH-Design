After the inevitable delays, the day has finally come for another release in the Gran Turismo series. Even with rivals heating things up in the sim racer category these days, I have always appreciated the extra polish and detail that Polyphony Digital adds. Gran Turismo Sport is no exception and does an incredible job squeezing all the power out of the Playstation 4.
